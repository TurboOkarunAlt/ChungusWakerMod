Chungus Waker

Welcome

Chungus Waker is a custom Wind Waker mod featuring Big Chungus as the Hero of Time and the 6–7 kid as the villain.

Download Mod

About the Mod

This mod is a parody-style reimagining of The Wind Waker. Expect cursed energy, memes, and unexpected character swaps.

Built for fun using Dolphin and custom assets. This is a fan-made project and not affiliated with Nintendo.
